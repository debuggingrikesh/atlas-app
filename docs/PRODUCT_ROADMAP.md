# Project Atlas Product Roadmap

## Vision

Become Nepal's leading customer experience intelligence platform for local businesses.

## MVP Goal

Help businesses collect customer feedback, recover unhappy customers, and improve operations.

## Phase 1
Foundation
Status: Complete

## Phase 2
Authentication + Business Setup

Goals:
- Business signup
- Owner account creation
- Business profile
- Branch creation
- Industry selection

## Phase 3
Feedback Engine

Goals:
- QR feedback page
- Rating collection
- Google review flow
- Private complaint capture

## Phase 4
AI Intelligence

Goals:
- Sentiment analysis
- Issue categorization
- AI summaries
- Recommendations

## Phase 5
Analytics

Goals:
- CX score
- Trends
- Reports
- Alerts
